- Global Smart TV shipment trends
- Major TV manufacturers: Samsung, TCL, Hisense, LG, Xiaomi and others

- Smart TV / Connected TV platform competition and OS sovereignty analysis

## Notes on Data Reliability

This dashboard combines officially disclosed figures, market research estimates, and analyst-derived assumptions. Measurement definitions may differ across sources.

For example:

- TV shipments may refer to sell-in or sell-through depending on the source.
- TV OS reach may refer to monthly active users, active devices, installed base, or households.
- Regional OS share may be estimated from multiple sources where direct official disclosure is unavailable.

そのため、本ダッシュボードでは、実績値・推計値・補完推計を区別しながら、可能な限り出典と前提を明記しています。
